Welcome!

Try and add red, green, and blue together to get the target colour. Note that this is additive (if you put them all at maximum you get white) rather than subtractive - think of it like shining three overlapping coloured lights at the same spot, rather than mixing paints.

To give some examples:

  • Bright pink is a lot of red, very little green, and a medium amount of blue
  • Sky blue is a medium amount of red, a lot of green, and a lot of blue
  • Yellow is a lot of red, a lot of green, and very little blue
  • Black is no red, no green, and no blue

    • Your highest score (up to a max of 500 for a perfect match) is the one that counts.

    The colours are on a scale from 0-255; this is based on the way that computers most commonly "see" colour - as a combination of red, green, and blue pixels.

    You can adjust the number of guesses you have, and the type of hints you get, in the settings menu.

    Good luck!
